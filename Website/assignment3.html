Assigment 1

Prototype Design

What is this??

With this Assignment, we tried to heal one of our school's bleeding wounds, the FHICT website. So far, we have learned to analyze problems and how to deal with them. Former assignments have guided us to understand how we can approach our new website. We drew a paper prototype before creating the new website. This saved us a lot of time and effort and showed us our shortcomings. After we finished our paper prototype and tested it on our fellow students, we decided to implement it. Not only does the new website look much better, it has also been improved in terms of UX.

Assigment 1

Creating Personas

What is this??

To determine how to start our project, we need to formulate a statement. We have been asked to research complaints about difficulties when using the FHICT portal. The FHICT portal is a place where students can get the more general information and materials required to study. So, it seems there are some shortcomings from the sitewhich are leading to complaints.The result of our project is to reach a set of advices on how to improve this portal.This leads us to the following statement:

To define shortcomings of the portal by finding out what ourtarget audience is and which requirements they have for using the portal.
